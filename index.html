What do we do?

We are HFHS -Alumni and we help others.

Holy Family High School -Alumni Giving privilege began its initiative of supporting Government Schools with infrastructure and basic requirements in 2016. Now into the fifth year and this team has very successfully completed 11 projects.

"Where we have reached today could not have been possible without the contributions from students both from India and overseas. A big thank you to each one for believing and joining hands with us. More power to all and let's always put our best foot forward."

Project 1

On the occasion of the 71st Republic day, January 26th 2020, HFHS-alumni has supported the Government Primary school prashanthi nagar malkajgiri by providing 30 units of desks and benches. Students very facing hardship sitting on the floor. keeping books on their laps and writing. Also due to dust and dirt their books and uniforms were getting soiled. Now the students are mighty thrilled and the teachers are very grateful and happy that the kids have at last got what they needed.
