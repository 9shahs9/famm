Project 1

On the occasion of the 71st Republic day, January 26th 2020, HFHS-alumni has supported the Government Primary school prashanthi nagar malkajgiri by providing 30 units of desks and benches.

How it helped

Students very facing hardship sitting on the floor. keeping books on their laps and writing. Also due to dust and dirt their books and uniforms were getting soiled. Now the students are mighty thrilled and the teachers are very grateful and happy that the kids have at last got what they needed.

They feel the children will now focus, concentrate better at the same time able to read and write with right posture. Its often said that Dream and your dreams will be realised. This initiative was our DREAM and seeing it grow year after year gives each one of us tremendous satisfaction , deep sense of pride and happy that we are in a space to be able to give.

All the schools in the umbrella of HOLY FAMILY ALUMNI -GIVING PRIVILEGE have pledged to make the school and classroom environment more conducive for learning, ensure students get the best of and work with commitment. They have observed that school attendance and admissions show healthy change. Parents are glad that their kids want to go to school. All these positive signs are encouragement for us to support more needy schools. Where we have reached today could not have been possible without the contributions from students both from India and overseas. A big thank you to each one for believing and joining hands with us. More power to all and let's always put our best foot forward.
