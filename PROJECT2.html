What did we do in Project 2

We have provided 40 units of desks and benches to ZPHS middle school subashnagar.

Children feel excited and enthused to come to school and one step towards that is providing the right atmosphere and infrastructure. HFHS ALUMNI has steadily strived to achieve this since 2016.A dozen ZP govt. schools in and around ALWAL area have benefited through our committed reach out initiative.

On the ocassion of our Nation's 73rd Independence day we are proud and happy to have supported the students. The twinkle in their eyes, broad grin and profuse thanks is reward enough to be motivated to take up the next project. The core group of HFHS ex-students have tirelessly spread the word and raised funds here and abroad. A huge thank you to each one for believing in the joy of GIVING a PREVILEGE.
